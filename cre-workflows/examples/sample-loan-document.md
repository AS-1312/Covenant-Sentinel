# SENIOR SECURED TERM LOAN AGREEMENT

**Dated:** 15 February 2024

---

## PARTIES

**Borrower:** Meridian Industrial Holdings Ltd.
**Lender:** Apex Credit Opportunities Fund III, L.P.
**Agent:** Apex Capital Management LLC
**Tokenisation Agent:** Covenant Sentinel Protocol

---

## ARTICLE I — LOAN TERMS

### 1.1 Principal Amount

The Lender agrees to advance to the Borrower a term loan in an aggregate principal amount of **USD 50,000,000** (fifty million United States Dollars) (the "Loan"). The Loan is represented by a tokenised position with ERC-20 token designation **MERIDIAN-TL-2024-A** deployed on Ethereum.

### 1.2 Interest Rate

The Loan bears interest at a fixed rate of **7.25% per annum**, payable quarterly in arrears on each Interest Payment Date.

### 1.3 Maturity Date

The Loan matures on **15 February 2028** (the "Maturity Date"), being 4 years from the date hereof.

### 1.4 Reporting Frequency

The Borrower shall deliver Compliance Certificates to the Agent on a **quarterly basis**, within 45 days of the end of each fiscal quarter. Covenant testing shall occur every **90 days**.

---

## ARTICLE II — CONDITIONS PRECEDENT

The obligation of the Lender to advance the Loan is subject to receipt of:

1. Audited financial statements for the two most recent fiscal years.
2. A duly executed Pledge Agreement over all material assets of the Borrower.
3. Evidence of insurance maintained in accordance with Article VI.
4. Legal opinions from counsel to the Borrower.

---

## ARTICLE III — FINANCIAL COVENANTS

The Borrower covenants and agrees that, from the date hereof until the Maturity Date, it shall at all times maintain compliance with each of the following financial covenants, tested quarterly as at the last day of each fiscal quarter:

### 3.1 Maximum Total Net Leverage Ratio

The **Total Net Leverage Ratio** shall not exceed **4.50x** at any testing date.

*"Total Net Leverage Ratio"* means the ratio of (a) Total Financial Indebtedness minus Unrestricted Cash and Cash Equivalents to (b) Adjusted EBITDA, in each case calculated on a trailing twelve-month basis.

*EBITDA Adjustments:* Adjusted EBITDA shall be calculated excluding (i) non-recurring restructuring charges up to USD 3,000,000 per annum, (ii) non-cash stock-based compensation expense, (iii) gains or losses from asset disposals outside the ordinary course of business, and (iv) transaction fees and costs related to acquisitions permitted under this Agreement.

### 3.2 Minimum Interest Coverage Ratio

The **Interest Coverage Ratio** shall not be less than **2.50x** at any testing date.

*"Interest Coverage Ratio"* means the ratio of Adjusted EBITDA to Total Cash Interest Expense, calculated on a trailing twelve-month basis.

*EBITDA Adjustments:* Same as defined in Section 3.1 above.

### 3.3 Minimum Debt Service Coverage Ratio

The **Debt Service Coverage Ratio** shall not be less than **1.20x** at any testing date.

*"Debt Service Coverage Ratio"* means the ratio of (a) Adjusted EBITDA minus Unfunded Capital Expenditure to (b) Total Debt Service (being scheduled principal amortisation plus Total Cash Interest Expense), calculated on a trailing twelve-month basis.

*EBITDA Adjustments:* Same as defined in Section 3.1 above. Additionally, budgeted maintenance capital expenditure may be excluded from Unfunded Capital Expenditure where supported by board-approved budgets.

### 3.4 Maximum Capital Expenditure

The Borrower's aggregate **Capital Expenditure** in any fiscal year shall not exceed **USD 8,000,000** (eight million United States Dollars).

*"Capital Expenditure"* means all expenditures by the Borrower for the acquisition of fixed or capital assets determined in accordance with GAAP, excluding (i) expenditure financed by permitted acquisition debt, and (ii) insurance proceeds reinvested in replacement assets within 180 days of receipt.

For the purposes of this covenant, Capital Expenditure is expressed as a ratio of trailing twelve-month Adjusted Revenue; the maximum permitted ratio is **0.12x** (12% of revenue).

### 3.5 Minimum Liquidity

The Borrower shall maintain, at all times, minimum **Unrestricted Cash and Cash Equivalents** of not less than **USD 5,000,000** (five million United States Dollars).

For covenant testing purposes, this is expressed as a ratio of Total Financial Indebtedness; the minimum permitted ratio is **0.10x** (10% of total debt).

*Adjustments:* Undrawn availability under committed revolving credit facilities with a remaining term of not less than 12 months may be included in the calculation of Unrestricted Cash for this purpose.

## ARTICLE IV — EVENTS OF DEFAULT

Each of the following constitutes an Event of Default:

(a) Failure to pay any principal amount when due.
(b) Failure to pay any interest or fees within five (5) Business Days of the due date.
(c) Breach of any financial covenant set forth in Article III that is not remedied within **30 days** of the earlier of (i) the Borrower becoming aware of such breach and (ii) the Agent delivering notice to the Borrower.
(d) A Material Adverse Effect occurs.
(e) Cross-default under any other Financial Indebtedness exceeding USD 5,000,000.
(f) Insolvency, bankruptcy, or analogous proceedings.

---

## ARTICLE V — REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

The Borrower represents and warrants that as of the date hereof:

1. It is duly incorporated and in good standing under the laws of its jurisdiction of formation.
2. It has full corporate power and authority to execute and perform this Agreement.
3. The execution of this Agreement does not violate any applicable law or contractual obligation.
4. No litigation is pending that would reasonably be expected to result in a Material Adverse Effect.
5. All financial statements delivered hereunder fairly present the financial condition of the Borrower.

---

## ARTICLE VI — AFFIRMATIVE COVENANTS

The Borrower shall:

(a) Deliver audited annual financial statements within 120 days of fiscal year end.
(b) Deliver quarterly management accounts within 45 days of each fiscal quarter end.
(c) Promptly notify the Agent of any Event of Default or Potential Event of Default.
(d) Maintain adequate insurance over all material assets.
(e) Preserve all material licences and permits.

---

## ARTICLE VII — NEGATIVE COVENANTS

The Borrower shall not, without prior written consent of the Agent:

(a) Incur any additional Financial Indebtedness exceeding USD 10,000,000 in aggregate.
(b) Create any Lien over its assets other than Permitted Liens.
(c) Make any acquisition with a consideration exceeding USD 15,000,000.
(d) Declare or pay dividends if an Event of Default is continuing or would result therefrom.

---
